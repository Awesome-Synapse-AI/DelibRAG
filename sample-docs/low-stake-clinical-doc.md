# Your Guide to Daily Wellness
**Simple Steps to a Healthier You**

---

## 💧 Stay Hydrated

Water is essential for every function in your body. Aim for 8 glasses (about 2 liters) per day, more if you're active or in hot weather.

**Quick tips:**
- Keep a reusable water bottle with you
- Drink a glass of water when you wake up
- If plain water is boring, add lemon, cucumber, or mint

---

## 🥗 Eat a Balanced Diet

Fill your plate with variety and color.

**The simple plate method:**
- **½ plate:** Vegetables and fruits
- **¼ plate:** Lean proteins (fish, chicken, beans, tofu)
- **¼ plate:** Whole grains (brown rice, quinoa, whole wheat)

**Remember:** Limit processed foods, excess sugar, and saturated fats.

---

## 🚶 Move Your Body Daily

You don't need a gym membership to stay active!

**Aim for:**
- 150 minutes of moderate activity per week (brisk walking, cycling)
- OR 75 minutes of vigorous activity (running, swimming)
- Strength training 2x per week

**Easy ways to add movement:**
- Take the stairs instead of the elevator
- Park farther away from store entrances
- Walk during phone calls
- Stretch every hour if you sit at a desk

---

## 😴 Prioritize Quality Sleep

Most adults need 7-9 hours of sleep per night.

**Better sleep habits:**
- Keep a consistent sleep schedule (even on weekends)
- Create a cool, dark, quiet bedroom
- Avoid screens 1 hour before bed
- Limit caffeine after 2 PM
- Wind down with reading or gentle stretching

---

## 🧘 Manage Stress

Chronic stress affects both mental and physical health.

**Try these techniques:**
- **Deep breathing:** 5 minutes of slow, deep breaths
- **Meditation or mindfulness:** Start with just 5 minutes daily
- **Connect with others:** Call a friend, join a community group
- **Time in nature:** Even 20 minutes outdoors helps
- **Hobbies and play:** Make time for activities you enjoy

---

## 🚭 Avoid Harmful Habits

- **Don't smoke** or use tobacco products
- **Limit alcohol** to moderate levels (up to 1 drink/day for women, 2 for men)
- **Avoid recreational drugs**

---

## 🩺 Stay Current with Preventive Care

Regular checkups catch problems early.

**Don't skip:**
- Annual physical exams
- Age-appropriate screenings (blood pressure, cholesterol, cancer screenings)
- Dental cleanings (every 6 months)
- Eye exams
- Vaccinations and boosters

---

## 🧼 Practice Good Hygiene

Simple habits prevent illness:
- Wash hands frequently (20 seconds with soap)
- Brush teeth twice daily and floss
- Shower regularly
- Keep living spaces clean

---

## 🧠 Keep Your Mind Active

Mental health is just as important as physical health.

**Brain-healthy activities:**
- Learn new skills or hobbies
- Read regularly
- Do puzzles or brain games
- Stay socially connected
- Seek professional help if you're struggling with mental health

---

## 🌞 Get Some Sunshine

Sunlight helps your body produce vitamin D and regulates your sleep cycle.

- Aim for 10-30 minutes of midday sun several times per week
- Protect your skin with sunscreen for longer exposure

---

## ❤️ Listen to Your Body

**Pay attention to warning signs:**
- Persistent pain or discomfort
- Unexplained weight changes
- Unusual fatigue
- Changes in mood or mental state

**When in doubt, consult your healthcare provider.**

---

## Start Small, Build Gradually

You don't need to change everything at once! Pick 1-2 habits to focus on this month. Small, consistent changes lead to lasting results.

---

### Remember: This brochure provides general wellness information only and is not a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult your healthcare provider for personalized recommendations.

**Your health is your most valuable asset. Invest in it daily!**
